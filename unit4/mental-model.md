# Unit 4 Mental Model — Industrial IoT Applications

---

## Topic Groups

### Group 1: IIoT in Healthcare
**Core idea:** Using IoT sensors and connected systems to monitor patients continuously, manage medical equipment, and improve hospital operations — bringing hospital-grade monitoring outside hospital walls.
**Questions:** Q1, Q5, Q18

### Group 2: IIoT in Power Plants & Energy Management
**Core idea:** Monitoring power plant equipment (turbines, boilers, transformers) with IoT sensors for predictive maintenance, real-time efficiency tracking, and energy optimization.
**Questions:** Q6, Q14, Q22

### Group 3: Inventory Management & Quality Control
**Core idea:** Using IoT (RFID, barcodes, sensors) to track materials and finished goods in real time, and deploying IIoT to monitor quality parameters during production to catch defects early.
**Questions:** Q3, Q7, Q8, Q22

### Group 4: Plant Safety, Security & Facility Management
**Core idea:** IIoT systems that monitor for hazardous conditions (gas leaks, fire, unauthorized access), track worker location and safety, and manage building infrastructure (HVAC, lighting, access).
**Questions:** Q2, Q4, Q9, Q10, Q11, Q17, Q19

### Group 5: IIoT Impact, Challenges & Data-Driven Decisions
**Core idea:** Evaluating the benefits of IIoT on industrial productivity, the challenges of deploying these systems at scale, ethical/security concerns, and how IIoT enables data-driven management decisions.
**Questions:** Q12, Q13, Q15, Q16, Q20, Q21
