# Unit 4: Industrial IoT Applications

## Low Difficulty – 20%
1. List applications of IIoT in healthcare.
2. Define plant safety systems.
3. What is inventory management?
4. Define facility management.

## Moderate Difficulty – 60%
5. Explain IIoT applications in healthcare monitoring.
6. Describe IIoT-based power plant monitoring systems.
7. Explain inventory management using IIoT.
8. Discuss IIoT applications in quality control.
9. Explain plant safety and security using IIoT.
10. Describe AR/VR applications in industrial safety.
11. Explain facility management using IIoT.
12. Analyze the benefits of IIoT in industrial automation.
13. Discuss challenges in deploying IIoT applications.
14. Explain energy management using IIoT.
15. Illustrate an IIoT application with a case study.
16. Explain data-driven decision-making in IIoT applications.

## High Difficulty – 20%
17. Design an IIoT-based safety system for an industrial plant.
18. Analyze the application of Industrial IoT (IIoT) in healthcare systems and evaluate how it improves patient monitoring and operational efficiency.
19. Propose an AR/VR-enabled safety training system.
20. Evaluate the impact of IIoT on industrial productivity.
21. Analyze ethical and security issues in IIoT applications.
22. Analyze how IIoT technology can be used in power plants and inventory management systems to enhance efficiency and reduce operational costs.
